(S//SI) New SID Senior Manager for Wireless
FROM:
SID/Deputy Director for Data Acquisition (S3)
Run Date: 06/03/2005

(S//SI) Ms. Teresa Shea has been appointed as the SID Senior Manager for Wireless. Ms. Shea
is replacing Mr. Fran Landolf, who recently retired from Government Service. In addition to the
Wireless Senior Manager duties, Ms. Shea is the Chief of Tailored Access Operations (TAO) group
-- the hub of NSA/CSS's endpoint access activity. Wireless encompasses those personal
communications devices offering mobility and location-based services such as cell phones,
satellite phones, trunk mobile radio, WLAN, and numerous other evolving wireless technologies.
(S//SI) The Wireless Portfolio Management Office will continue under Ms. Shea's leadership to
implement the Wireless Strategy throughout the Intelligence Community, to include our Second
Party partners. Please see additional information about the SID Wireless Strategy and
