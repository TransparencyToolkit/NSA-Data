(U) Have you been wondering how to identify, prioritize, and document current and future
SIGINT capabilities? The answers you need are now available in the updated version of SID
Management Directive 401, "SIGINT Capabilities Development System," dated 9 June 2005.
This document is accessible on the NSA/CSS Intranet via this link:
SID Directive 401
...or by typing "go sigint-policy" and clicking on the appropriate hyperlink.

efforts, regardless of cost.
