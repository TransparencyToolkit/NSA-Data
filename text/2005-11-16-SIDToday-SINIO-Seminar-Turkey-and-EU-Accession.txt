(U//FOUO) SINIO Seminar: Turkey and EU Accession
FROM: Marilyn Maines
Deputy Chief, Strategic Intelligence Issues (S17)
Run Date: 11/16/2005

(U//FOUO) On Monday, 21 November 2005 the SINIO Council and the National Cryptologic
School are pleased to present the seminar "Turkey and EU Accession," delivered by the SINIO
for Europe,
on Monday, 21 November from 1130 to 1300 in room 2W037-2.
Advance registration through the NCS is required. (AREA-1500)
Course Overview:
(U//FOUO) This seminar will focus upon the current status of the European Union (EU) and how
that status has been impacted by the prospect of Turkish membership. It will also address
related cultural, religious, and geopolitical issues for EU members, should Turkey's quest for
membership become a reality.
