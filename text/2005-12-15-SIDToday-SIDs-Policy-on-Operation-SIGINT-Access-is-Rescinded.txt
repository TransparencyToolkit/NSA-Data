(U//FOUO) SID's Policy on Operation SIGINT Access is Rescinded
FROM: 
Deputy Chief, SIGINT Policy (S02L1)
Run Date: 12/15/2005

(U//FOUO) At the request of the Operation SIGINT (OpSIGINT) Lead, on 15 November 2005
SIGINT Policy (S02L1) rescinded the "Signals Intelligence Policy on Operation SIGINT ECI
Access," which was dated 14 February 2002.
(U//FOUO) OpSIGINT has determined that its new operating construct, which involves the
establishment of Memorandums of Agreement and Statements of Work with stakeholders, makes
the 2002 policy superfluous. The point of contact for this subject is 
OpSIGINT Lead,
